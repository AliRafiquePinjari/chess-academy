Our Training Program

LEVEL I

For Absolute Beginners

Duration: 24 Sessions (3 Months)

This level covers chess basics:

  • Game rules
  • Piece movements
  • Special moves like castling
  • Check and checkmate
  • Chess notations

Perfect for beginners to get started!

LEVEL II

For Advanced Beginners

Duration: 24 Sessions (3 Months)

This module focuses on:

  • Master checkmate techniques
  • Winning with material advantage
  • Proper exchanges
  • Opening strategies
  • Common checkmating ideas
LEVEL III

For Intermediate Chess Players

Duration: 40 Sessions (6 Months)

This module focuses on:

  • Checkmate with 1 Rook
  • Checkmate with Double Bishops
  • Intro to Chess Tactics & Piece Combinations
  • Pin, Knight Fork, Double Attack
  • Skewer Attack, Back Rank
  • Discovered Attack, Discovered Check
  • Destroying the Defender
LEVEL IV

For Advanced Chess Players

Duration: 48 Sessions (6 Months)

This module focuses on:

  • The Concept of Opposition
  • Rule of the Square
  • King & Pawn Endings
  • Queen v/s Pawn
  • Knight v/s Pawn
  • Rook v/s Pawn
  • Queen v/s Rook
  • Fundamentals of Rook Endings
LEVEL V

For Advanced Chess Players

Duration: 48 Sessions (6 Months)

This module focuses on:

  • Elementary Checkmate:
    Bishop & Knight
  • Checkmating Patterns
  • Intro to Chess Tactics &
    Piece Combinations
  • Decoy, Deflection, Overloaded Piece
  • Line Opening & Closing,
    Square Vacation
  • Passed Pawn, Zwischenzug
  • Drawing Combinations, X-Ray Attack
  • Windmills
